Regatta Bar

Taking it's name from Carrick on Shannon's rich history in rowing and in particular the annual Carrick on Shannon Regatta which takes place on the Sunday of the August Bank Holiday weekend and indeed the owner Frances Cryan's own personal involvement in rowing, having represented Ireland in the 1980 Moscow Olympics.

The Regatta Bar serves food daily starting with Carvery Lunch at 12.30 and running start through to 21.00.
